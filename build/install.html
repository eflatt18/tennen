1. UNBOXING

Your Tennen Kit Includes

36 modules
1 wooden frame
36 nutrient packs
1 Algae Culture
1 harvesting tool
4 drywall anchors
4 drywall screws

2. MOUNTING THE FRAME

Start by drilling the holes

TIP: Your algae will need partial sun, make sure to pick a location on your wall that will get plenty of natural light

3. FILLING THE MODULES

Remove the lids and fill the modules with water up to the indentation mark on the side of the module. Add one nutrient packet and one algae culture vial

Algae reduces your
carbon footprint

4. PLACE MODULES IN THE FRAME

Place one module into each shelf of the frame, vent side up. Make sure the air circulator is connected properly.

5. CONGRATULATIONS NOW WE WAIT

Your algae will be at peak maturity when the module pops out, after about one month of growth
