HOW TO HARVEST

You will see your first module pop out after one month of introducing the culture. Tennen takes care of most of the work so you can relax and enjoy your fresh air!

1. wait for the module to pop out of the shelf

2. remove the module from the system

3. drain the water out of the module, using the lid as a strainer to catch the algae

4. use the remaining algae for your preferred use! see below for some ideas

5. refill the container with filtered water and add one packet of nutrients into the module

TIP: make sure to leave a dime sized amount of
algae in the container for future propogation
