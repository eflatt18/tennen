HOW TENNEN WORKS

Algae needs sunlight, nutrients, and air circulation to flourish- thats it! The built-in algae health monitor continuously relays information so that you stay informed about your algae’s growth.

Algae loves sunlight, the more the better. Make sure Tennen is placed in a partial-sun location.

Tennen’s nutrient subscription continuously keeps your algae fed so that you can grow algae efficiently and reap all the benefits.

Tennen circulates air within the modules to facilitate movement and growth.

HOW TENNEN WORKS

Nutrient refills and harvesting are the only maintenance you have to do to keep algae happy and healthy. The Tennen system does all the rest! Make sure your subscription plan matches your frequency of algae harvesting to maximise Tennen’s effectiveness.
